# Product

## Register

product

## Users

FounderOS is for online business founders and lean operators who need to understand sales, stock, customer feedback, chats, and order risk quickly during a live business day. They are deciding what to restock, which customer issue to answer first, which product needs attention, and what action will protect revenue.

## Product Purpose

FounderOS turns e-commerce JSON data into an AI-assisted command center that explains what is happening in the business and what the founder should do next. Success means the dashboard surfaces revenue health, operational risks, customer voice, and concrete next actions without forcing the user to inspect raw tables.

## Brand Personality

Decisive, trustworthy, founder-grade. The interface should feel like a calm operating room for a fast-moving online shop: data-forward, polished, practical, and confident without feeling flashy or gimmicky.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic analytics dashboards that only show charts without decisions. Avoid crypto-terminal darkness, decorative AI gradients, oversized marketing hero sections, childish illustrations, and UI that hides important operational states behind novelty.

## Design Principles

1. Decisions before decoration: every surface should answer what happened, why it matters, and what to do next.
2. Local data earns trust: AI and fallback insights must cite real business signals from orders, products, reviews, chats, and stock.
3. Demo reliability is a feature: fallback, offline, cached, and simulated states must be obvious and instant.
4. Dense but calm: founders need scan-friendly information hierarchy, not visual noise.
5. Mobile is operational: the app must remain usable on a phone during a live demo or store check-in.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA-level contrast and keyboard-accessible controls. Respect reduced motion, avoid color-only status communication, keep tables responsive, and ensure offline, fallback, loading, empty, and error states are readable on small screens.
